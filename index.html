Definition

A simple hypothesis is a testable statement that predicts a directional relationship between two variables only: one independent variable (cause) and one dependent variable (effect).

5 Nursing Examples (Simple Style)

1. The higher the nurse-to-patient ratio, the lower the patient satisfaction scores.
2. The more frequent the hand hygiene compliance, the fewer hospital-acquired infections occur.
3. The earlier the ambulation after surgery, the shorter the hospital stay.
4. The greater the sleep quality in ICU nurses, the lower their reported burnout levels.
5. The more consistent the wound care documentation, the faster the healing time in diabetic foot ulcers.
